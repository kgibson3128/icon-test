Icon test page

If the home screen icon is working, you should see the rose icon when you add this page to your iPhone home screen.

How to add to home screen

1
Open this page in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2
Tap the Share button (box with arrow) at the bottom
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add — you should see the rose icon on your home screen
